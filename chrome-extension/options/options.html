Setup Guide

First Time Setup

  1. Click "Sign in with Google" above
  2. Allow access to your Google Calendar
  3. Select which calendars to show
  4. Click the extension icon to view your calendar

Features

  • View your calendar in a compact popup
  • See upcoming events for the next 7 days
  • Click events to open them in Google Calendar
  • Badge shows current date
  • Video call indicators for Zoom, Meet, and Teams
